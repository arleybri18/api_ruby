Meet Auto-Task

Features

Copy

Auto-Task is a useful tool for those who need to collect information every day from somewhere in the net. For example, tables that are changing over the time, with our tool, people can save steps one time and execute it as many times as they need. Saving time to do a more efficient work.

Write

Sometimes the repetitive task is about to fill-up some formulary with the same information every time. Why not doing that one time, saving that steps and execute it when needed. Auto-Task is for you.

Click

Always a click is needed when you are looking for something right there in the web. Auto-Task is even doing that click for you. Save it, execute it, and don't ever worry about those tedious clicks anymore.
